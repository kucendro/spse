Kucendr Observatory Orel

Observatory tours

Our observatory offers guided tours of the night sky, where you can learn about the stars and planets from our expert astronomers. Our tours are suitable for all ages and levels of experience.

Stargazing events

We also host stargazing events throughout the year, where you can come and observe the night sky through our telescopes. Our events are suitable for all ages and levels of experience.

Telescope rentals

If you want to observe the night sky on your own, we offer telescope rentals for a small fee. Our telescopes are easy to use and come with instructions on how to set them up and use them.

Modern technology

Telescopes

Our observatory is equipped with the latest telescopes to help you see the stars and planets in the night sky. We have a variety of telescopes to suit all levels of experience, from beginners to experts.

Cameras

We also have a range of cameras to help you capture the beauty of the night sky. Our cameras are designed to work with our telescopes to give you the best possible images of the stars and planets.

Computers

Our observatory is equipped with the latest computers to help you analyze the data you collect from the night sky. Our computers are designed to work with our telescopes and cameras to give you the best possible results.
